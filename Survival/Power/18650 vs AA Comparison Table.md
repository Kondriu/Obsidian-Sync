
|Feature|18650 Battery|AA Battery|
|---|---|---|
|**Type**|Rechargeable Lithium-ion|Alkaline/NiMH/Li-ion|
|**Voltage**|3.7V (Nominal)|1.5V (Alkaline)|
|**Size**|18mm x 65mm|14mm x 50mm|
|**Capacity**|1800 - 3500mAh|600 - 2700mAh|
|**Application**|High-drain (Flashlights, EV)|Low-drain (Toys, Remotes)|


Key Takeaways

- **Do not substitute:** You cannot use an 18650 in an AA device, or vice-versa, without special adapters, as the voltage difference can damage electronics.

- **Capacity:** 18650 batteries offer far superior battery life for high-drain applications.

- **Power:** 18650 delivers higher, more consistent voltage, making them ideal for high-performance flashlights and tools
